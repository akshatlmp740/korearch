Welcome to Korearch Infrastructure Pvt Ltd

Korearch Infrastructure is your trusted partner in delivering excellence for medium and large-scale projects across industrial, commercial, and residential sectors. We specialize in steel structures, civil works, premium villas, and modular pods, ensuring precision, durability, and innovation in every build.

Our expertise spans hybrid steel structures, light gauge steel framing (LGSF), and turnkey developments, integrating cutting-edge engineering with superior craftsmanship. From substructure-civil work to fabrication, PEB (Pre-Engineered Buildings), façade, and roofing, we offer end-to-end solutions that redefine quality and efficiency.

With a commitment to sustainability, on-time delivery, and future-ready infrastructure, we transform visions into reality. Partner with us and build with confidence.
